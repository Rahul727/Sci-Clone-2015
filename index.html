About Sci - Clone

We are organizing a national level technical event under the name of “SciClone” every year. This symposium is carried under the objective to enhance and update the technical skills and knowledge of our students.
This year, we have designed this event, keeping in mind, the advancement of research and technologies in engineering areas. Thus, we plan to organize maximum number of exhibitions, seminars, and project presentations to attract a large number of engineering students on and off campus.
